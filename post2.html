Brand new pants!

Thanks to Bawbags, we're ready to say PANTS to feeling rubbish, with our brand new Empowerment Pants!
, Dec 7, 2017




These are no ordinary pants… 100% of profits from Empowerment Pants will go towards funding places on our empowerment and wellbeing initiatives for women and girls on low-income.

Self-care and wellbeing is for everyone, and by selling Empowerment Pants we can ensure that every woman is able to access and benefit from our support.

So, if you're looking for a gift for someone special, or if you fancy feeling like a superhero, then these are the undies for you!
Support female empowerment and get your pair!
